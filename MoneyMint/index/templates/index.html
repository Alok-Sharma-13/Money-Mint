How you can reduce Carbon footprint

Reducing your carbon footprint is important, and there are simple steps to make a difference:

  • Use public transport or carpool to reduce emissions from personal vehicles.
  • Turn off lights and unplug devices when not in use to conserve energy.
  • Reduce paper waste by going digital wherever possible, such as opting for e-receipts.
  • Follow the 3 R's: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, to cut down on waste generation.
  • Support local products and eco-friendly brands to minimize transportation impact.
  • Opt for energy-efficient appliances and consider renewable energy sources.
